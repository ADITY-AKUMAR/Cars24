Objective
• Provide leadership a concise overview of 
customer sentiment on sell-side
• Analyze online mentions across social 
platforms and forums
• Evaluate effectiveness of ticket resolution and 
winback process
• Identify key customer pain points and 
improvement areas
Data & Assumptions
• Dummy data created for assessment purposes
• Time period: December 2024
• One row represents one customer mention
• Tickets created mainly for negative mentions
• Winback means issue resolved or post 
corrected
• TAT measured in hours

Key Insights
• Payment and pickup delays are major 
complaint drivers
• Winback rate around 54% shows scope for 
improvement
• Instagram and Facebook generate highest 
ORM volume
• Higher TAT leads to lower CSAT
• Duplicate mentions highlight unresolved 
issues
Recommendations
• Reduce payment and pickup turnaround time
• Improve RC transfer and documentation 
process
• Focus ORM efforts on high-volume platforms
• Proactively handle repeat complaints
